Apostrophe Master Challenge

Review the three rules below before you begin!

Rule 1

Singular Noun

To form the possessive of a single noun, add an ’ and an s.

Robert’s cat
the fan’s cord
Rule 2

Plural Ending in 'S'

To form the possessive of a plural noun ending in s, add only the ’.

the campers’ tent
two dogs’ tracks
Rule 3

Plural NOT Ending in 'S'

To form the possessive of a plural noun that does NOT end in s, add an ’ and an s.

mice’s cage
children’s toys
