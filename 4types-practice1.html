Ready to be a Sentence Sleuth?

Review the 4 types of sentences below. When you start the game, you'll need to identify the sentence type and choose the missing punctuation!

Declarative (.)

Statement: You are stating or declaring something.

"The heart of a shrimp is located in its head."

Exclamatory (!)

Exclamation: You are stating or exclaiming with emotion.

"That birthday party was so much fun!"

Interrogative (?)

Question: You are wondering or interrogating.

"Why do I always forget where I put my car?"

Imperative (. or !)

Demand: You are demanding something. It starts with a verb.

"Come fold your laundry."
