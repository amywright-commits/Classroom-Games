Who was Mother Goose?
"Mother Goose" isn't a real person, but a fictional author associated with French fairy tales and English nursery rhymes. The name dates back to the 1600s! These silly, strange, and sometimes dark poems were passed down orally for hundreds of years before ever being written in a book.
