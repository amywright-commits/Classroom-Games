Spicy Apostrophe Challenge!

Can you handle the heat? Review the 5 spicy rules before you begin!

🌶️
Spice Level 1

Tricky Contractions

Apostrophes replace missing letters in mashed-up words.

can not ➔ can't
it is ➔ it's
🌶️
Spice Level 2

Singular Words Ending in S

Even if a single noun ends in S, usually you still add 's.

the boss's desk
Chris's shoes
🌶️🌶️
Spice Level 3

Regular Plurals

If a plural word ends in S, just add the apostrophe ' at the end.

ten dogs' bones
the teachers' lounge
🌶️🌶️🌶️
Spice Level 4

Irregular Plurals

If a plural word does NOT end in S, you must add 's.

the children's toys
the mice's cheese
🔥
Maximum Spice

The Pronoun Trap

Possessive pronouns NEVER use apostrophes. They already show ownership!

its (belongs to it)
whose (belongs to who)
yours, ours, theirs
👻
Ghost Pepper

The Imposter Plural

If a word is just plural (more than one) and DOESN'T own anything, use NO apostrophe!

three cats
many cars
