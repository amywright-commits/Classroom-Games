Adjective Adventure!

An adjective describes a noun. It tells us what the person, place, thing, or idea is LIKE.
It's a way to make the sentence better—just like decorations on a cupcake!

Notice how the adjective can move around!
  • 🧁 The hungry boy ate the cupcake.
  • 🧁 Hungry, the boy ate the cupcake.
  • 🧁 The boy, hungry, ate the cupcake.
  • 🧁 The boy was hungry.
