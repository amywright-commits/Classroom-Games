What is a noun?

a noun is a person, place, thing, or idea.

The Rules:

  • ✨ a proper noun is a specific name and needs a capital letter (like Nike or Starbucks).
  • 📦 a common noun is general and does not need a capital (like shoe or store).
  • 💡 an idea noun is a value or feeling you can’t touch, like bravery or friendship.
